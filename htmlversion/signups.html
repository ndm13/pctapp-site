Competition Ground Rules

Teams will consist of 1-3 members who will compete at the highest member's skill level.

There are two skill levels:
Low Tier: Those who have not completed a 200-level programming course
High Tier: Those who have completed a 200-level programming course

You may bring paper and pencil apart from your one allowed computing device.

Problems will be provided via packet and web. Problems will be submitted via web.

Students may compete in higher tiers, but not lower.

Graduate students may compete, but not for prizes.

Only one computing device is allowed per team.
